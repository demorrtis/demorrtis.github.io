You head over to the people taking care of the fire. From what you see, the building isn't going to make it. What caused this?
It takes you a while to get the attention of some of the firefighters. They just tell you to step back and head to safety.

You assume the worst now that Haley is nowhere in sight. You retreat from the scene. You can't be around it anymore. As you're walking away you feel a tap on your shoulder. You turn to find your friend who called you earlier, he seems out of breath. "I got here as fast as I could!" You let him calm down before he could speak again.

" I don't know if you remember but I - I can send you back if you're willing," Your memory's a blur. You might have remembered something like this. " I can't go back, but I can send you back. Only thing is that if I end you back, you'll feel a little more exhausted and drained than before." Part of you wants to find a way to save Haley, but this other part of you feels like you can't do it.
