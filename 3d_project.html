About the 3D Lanscape


For this 3d project, I created an imaginary setting based off of real ancient pyramids
inspired by the pyramids from the animated film The Road to El Dorado .
I used an already existing 3D model for the pyramid and modified them to have hold similar details as those in the movie.
A body of water and small buildings on the side of the water were part of the setting. Bc of the symmetry these
structures held, it made it possible to easily build them up. Printing this model didn't come out
as perfectly planned, but there were ways around to be able to fix it - such as creating a separete
model of the smaller buildings on the side to later add to the larger printed base.
