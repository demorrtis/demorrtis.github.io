Remix with Sound

This montage is to represent how digital media today has taken over the lives of many. Today it seems as if people need new technology as much as they need water to live. It becomes a day to day need for some and the idea of going a few days without it is outrageous.
The sounds adds into the video gives it a eerie mood in this strange setting.
